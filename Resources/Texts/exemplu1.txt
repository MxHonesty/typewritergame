IN CERTAIN OF THE SANDSTONES,
DYNAMITED AND CHISELED AFTER BORING
REVEALED THEIR NATURE, WE FOUND
SOME HIGHLY INTERESTING FOSSIL MARKINGS
AND FRAGMENTS; NOTABLY FERNS, SEAWEEDS,
TRILOBITES, CRINOIDS, AND SUCH MOLLUSKS
AS LINGUELLAE AND GASTROPODS - ALL
OF WHICH SEEMED OF REAL
SIGNIFICANCE IN CONNECTION WITH THE
REGION'S PRIMORDIAL HISTORY.
